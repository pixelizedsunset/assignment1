Ribbit, Ribbit!


This web page is dedicated to humanity's favorite amphibian: the humble frog! Here at The Frog Appreciation Page, we aim to remedy the tragic reality that frogs, though incredible, often do not receive the love and attention that they deserve in their day-to-day lives. How? By putting the spotlight on our favorite frogs!


Every week, our team ranks local frogs to highlight the diverse magnificence found in order Anura. This week's top three overall best frogs are listed below! To read their bios, scroll below, or use the button directory to "hop" to any particular ranking! Have fun!


Check back weekly for a new set of best frogs!

#3 Frog of the Week

"Arnold"

Species: American Bullfrog
Gender: Male
Personality: Quiet


Arnold can be a little shy, but once you get to know him, he'll be your truest life-long friend. He enjoys eating moths and long walks on the pavement. The only reason he was not ranked higher was because the way he stares at people can be a little off-putting, but maybe he'll make it to #1 Frog next week!

#2 Frog of the Week

"Majora"

Species: Barking Treefrog
Gender: Female
Personality: Fiesty!


Majora is the definition of small but mighty! Despite her petite frame, Majora is a perpetually-angry, ready-to-rumble frog. Even though she is rude, she has earned this week's #2 Frog rank because she ate a bug that was terrorizing one of our web managers. A guardian angel!

#1 Frog of the Week

"Mr. Grumpy"

Species: Blanchard's Cricket Frog
Gender: Male
Personality: Gentle


Mr. Grumpy has a very unfortunate name, for he is not grumpy in the slightest. In fact, if you speak with him, you'll find that he is perhaps the kindest soul in amphibian history! Mr. Grumpy is fascinated by mushrooms and often suns himself in our lead web developer's garden. When asked what his favorite type of mushroom is, he responded with the following: "Croak ribbit ribbit croak croak." Unfortunately, our translator was away for the weekend and we were unable to understand what he meant by this.
